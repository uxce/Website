BIOGRAPHY
Hey, I'm Alex. I'm a 20-year-old IT student at the University of Central Florida with an interest in cybersecurity. I got into technology simply because I grew up around computers and always wanted to know how they worked. Whether it was building PCs, fixing random issues, or taking things apart just to understand them, I've always enjoyed learning how systems work behind the scenes.
In 2025, I competed in Horse Plinko which is a blue team cybersecurity competition hosted by HackUCF. It gave me the opportunity to apply what I've learned in a fast-paced environment while working alongside other students who share the same interests. My team placed 4th overall.
When I'm not working on technology, I'm usually working on cars. My biggest project right now is my 1995 Nissan 240SX (s14) The gallery on this site documents some of that journey, from maintenance and repairs to the long-term plans I have for the car.
The retro amber color scheme used throughout this website is inspired by 1990s dashboard lighting and instrument clusters. It's a small detail, but one that reflects both my interest in older Japanese cars and my appreciation for thoughtful design.
